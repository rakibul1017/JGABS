About the Journal

Journal of Global Arts, Business and Sciences (JGABS)

Overview

The Journal of Global Arts, Business and Sciences (JGABS) is an international, peer-reviewed, and open-access journal committed to advancing scholarly research in the fields of arts, business, and sciences. It is published twice a year (March & September).

Aims and Scope

JGABS seeks to promote interdisciplinary knowledge by publishing original research articles, reviews, and case studies that address contemporary issues and innovations across disciplines. Topics covered include, but are not limited to:

  • Arts, literature, and cultural studies
  • Business, management, and economics
  • Science, technology, and applied innovations
  • Education, linguistics, and social sciences

Publication Ethics

JGABS upholds the highest standards of academic integrity. We strictly follow ethical guidelines for authors, editors, and reviewers, ensuring transparency, originality, and academic honesty in every publication.

Open Access Policy

All articles published in JGABS are freely accessible online, ensuring knowledge dissemination without barriers. Authors retain copyright under the Creative Commons Attribution License.
